Frequently Asked Questions

How do I mix sounds?

Tap any sound to add it to your mix, then open the Now Playing screen to adjust each sound's volume independently. You can layer up to 10 sounds at once and save your favorite combinations as custom mixes.

How does the sleep timer work?

Open the Timer tab, choose a duration (from 5 minutes to 2 hours), and tap Start. Playback fades out gently when the timer ends.

Does it keep playing when my screen is off?

Yes. Somna continues playing in the background and with the screen locked, so it can play all night.

What is Somna Premium?

Somna Premium unlocks the full sound library and unlimited mixing. It is an auto-renewable subscription available annually, monthly, or weekly, with a 7-day free trial on the annual plan. Payment is charged to your Apple ID, and subscriptions renew automatically unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.

How do I restore my subscription on a new device?

Open Settings in the app and tap Restore Purchases, or simply sign in with the same Apple ID — your subscription is recognized automatically.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Go to your device Settings > Apple Account > Subscriptions, select Somna, and manage or cancel there.
