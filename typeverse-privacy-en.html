TypeVerse Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-08· Effective: 2026-06-08· Version: v1

Beijing Rixuan Information Technology Co., Ltd. ("we", "us", or the "Developer") is the developer of the "TypeVerse" personality-test app (the "App"). We take your privacy seriously.

The App runs entirely on your device. It has no backend server, requires no account or sign-in, and does not collect, upload, or store any of your personal information. All of your test answers, results, and history are stored only on your own device.

By using the App, you acknowledge that you have read and agree to this Policy. If you do not agree, please stop using the App.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • The App requires no registration, no login, and no phone number, email, or name.
  • Your answers, personality-type results, dimension scores, and history are stored only locally on your device (iOS UserDefaults / Android SharedPreferences) and are never uploaded to us or any third party.
  • We operate no servers, so we are technically unable to receive or retain your data.
  • The App contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or user-tracking SDKs. We do not profile you or track you across apps.

2. Device Permissions

The App requests the following permissions only when you actively use the related feature:

Photo Library (add-only)

When you tap "Save image" on the share-card screen, the App requests permission to write the personality card you generated to your photo library. We only write that single image you chose to save; we never read, browse, or upload any other photos. Declining this permission only disables "save to album".

System Share

When you tap "Share", the App invokes the native iOS / Android share sheet; you choose the destination app. We do not handle or retain any shared data.

3. In-App Purchases

Paid report unlocks are offered through Apple App Store In-App Purchase (Google Play Billing on Android).

  • All payments are processed entirely by Apple / Google via their native payment sheet.
  • We never access, collect, or store your card number, Apple ID password, or any payment credentials.
  • After a successful purchase, the unlock status and order record (type, version, amount, time, transaction ID) are stored only locally on your device, shown to you under "My orders".
  • Your purchases and refunds are governed by Apple's / Google's terms and privacy policies.

4. Storage & Deletion

  • Because data lives only on your device, deleting the App removes all local data (test history, order records, etc.).
  • You may also clear the App's data in system settings to remove all local records.

5. Children

The App is not directed at children. As we collect no personal information, no information about children is gathered. Minors should use the App with a guardian's consent.

6. About the Results

Personality types and report content are generated from publicly known personality models such as MBTI, and are for self-exploration and entertainment only — not a clinical diagnosis, medical advice, or basis for career decisions.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Updates are posted on this page with a revised date and version number; material changes will be communicated in-app.
